Attack Trees

Decompose an attacker goal into AND/OR sub-goals and leaf attacks (Schneier-style attack trees). XORCISM rolls up each leaf's feasibility — OR takes the easiest child, AND the hardest required child — to a root feasibility and the single easiest attack path. Mark a node mitigated to see the path collapse. Trees live in XORCISM.ATTACKTREE and can be tied to a THREATMODEL.
