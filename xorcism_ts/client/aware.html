AWARE — Autonomous AI-Agent Governance

Integration of AWARE (GoodCISO/aware, Apache-2.0) — autonomous-compliance infrastructure for AI agents — over XORCISM's discovered AI agents. Each agent is governed at a constraint-enforcement tier (T0 audit-only → T4 autonomous-under-constraints), carries a cryptographic non-human identity that can't be spoofed, and sits in a parent→child hierarchy with a revocation cascade (a kill-switch — revoking a parent automatically disables every child). Each tier maps to CSA AICM · NIST AI RMF · ISO 27001 · DORA · OWASP-LLM. Enforcement of the T2/T3 policy constraints runs in the Agent Policy Firewall; AWARE here is the governance posture & cascade authority.
